Who We Are

About Us

Drug invent is a medical technology and digital solution company focused on improving medical deliveries and therapeutic outcomes.
We work across the healthcare supply chain with the aim of building resilience using technology to ensure the right medication is always available and accessible at the right quality and quantity. Our objectives is to improve therapeutic outcomes such that patients gets the best and cost-effective therapeutic outcomes from healthcare providers.
Our digital solutions are Good Pharmacy Practice focused. We aim at strengthening supply chain efficiency, eliminating the risk of substandards/counterfeit products and easing the financial cost burden of medications procurement and management on health institutions.
